As I studied how to align my actions with my priorities, I quickly came across the word Ikigai. Which has become a helpful organizer in my life.

Ikigai is a Japanese concept meaning “a reason for being.” It represents the intersection of what you love, what you're good at, what the world needs, and what you can be paid for. When all four areas overlap, you find your purpose — your ikigai.


This video is from Yes Theory — one of my favorite YouTube channels that's all about seeking discomfort and living intentionally. It’s actually the first time I ever heard the word “Ikigai.” The way they explained it really stuck with me and got me thinking about how to live with more purpose.
